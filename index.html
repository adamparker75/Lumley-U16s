Lumley Ladies F.C

Under 16's

The Club

Lumley Ladies F.C. were formed 16 years ago, and have over 120 players from age 6 through to adults. Everyone at the Club works hard to provide a safe and fun environment for girls of all ages to play football and develop their skills. We currently have seven junior teams playing in the Russell Foster Youth Leagues on Saturday mornings and two adult teams who play in the Durham County League on Sunday afternoons.

The Team

Lumley Ladies Under 16's are members of the Russell Foster junior football league. They play their home games on Saturday mornings, at Burnside WMC, Fencehouses. The majority of the team have played together for the last 5 years, and are developing into a great team both on and off the pitch. The coach is Mark Wilkinson, who has overseen their development for the last 4 years.
